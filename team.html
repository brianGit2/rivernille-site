Moses Olang' Obiero

Chief Executive Officer

Moses leads RiverNille Construction with a vision rooted in precision, legacy, and transformative infrastructure. His leadership blends strategic clarity with poetic ambition — building not just structures, but enduring impact across East Africa.

With over a decade of experience in construction management and business development, Moses has spearheaded projects ranging from luxury residential developments to large-scale commercial complexes, always maintaining an unwavering commitment to quality and client satisfaction.
